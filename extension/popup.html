Welcome to the Heroes Draft Helper!

This tool is designed to help with the draft process for your Heroes of the Storm game. In order to operate, you'll need to Collect some data from HotsLogs.com using the buttons below. You can re-visit any of the pages feeds to update to the most current data, but at a minimum you'll need to load the Maps data, the Hero Sub-Role data, and at least one Player's win rates using the buttons below. Each Button will take you to the appropriate HotsLogs location, and you'll see a new button embedded on that page to "Store" that data. Be sure to load your friends' Player data, too, and update as needed!
